Starfield Lab

Project Struggles:

For me, the hardest part of the starfield lab was figuring out how to calculate the path of the particles. By using the formula (cos(angle)*speed) for the x position's movement and (sin(angle)*speed) for the y position's movement, I was able to get the particles to move in the correct manner.
