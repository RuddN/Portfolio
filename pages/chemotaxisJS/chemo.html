Chemotaxis Lab

Project Struggles:

For me, the hardest part of the Chemotaxis Lab was figuring out how to move the mice without them speeding off into the distance. I initially used ints as my variable for x, y, speedx, and speedy, which didn't allow for incremental enough changes. Using floats helped fix that problem.
